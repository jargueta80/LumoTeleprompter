# Privacy Policy for Lumo Teleprompter

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Overview

Lumo Teleprompter ("the App") is a free, open-source teleprompter application designed for content creators, presenters, and anyone who needs to read text while recording or presenting.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely offline and locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

### What the App Stores Locally

- **Scripts:** Your written scripts are stored only on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage).
- **Settings:** Your preferences (theme, font size, speed, etc.) are stored locally on your device.
- **Color Presets:** Custom color presets you create are stored locally on your device.

### What the App Does NOT Collect

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports
- No location data
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or tracking pixels

## Network Usage

The App uses local network connectivity **only** for the Remote Control feature:

- **Purpose:** To allow a second device on the same Wi-Fi network to control the teleprompter.
- **Scope:** Communication happens exclusively within your local network (LAN).
- **No Internet Required:** The Remote Control feature works without internet access.
- **No Data Sent Externally:** No data leaves your local network.

### Permissions Required

| Permission | Platform | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| `INTERNET` | Android | Required for local WebSocket communication |
| `ACCESS_WIFI_STATE` | Android | Required to get local IP address for remote control |
| `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | Android | Required to check network connectivity |
| `Local Network` | iOS | Required for device-to-device communication |

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The only external link in the App is an optional donation link to PayPal, which opens in your device's browser. No data is shared with PayPal through the App.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age. Since the App does not collect any data from any users, it is safe for use by all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Your data is as secure as your device itself
- Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data
- We cannot access, recover, or restore your data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
